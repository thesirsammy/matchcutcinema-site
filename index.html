Reflecting the Light
Faith Kazim is a director and screenwriter pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in filmmaking. Among other achievements, she's been crucially involved in numerous short films that have gone on to win Viddys and AVA Digital Awards. She loves telling redemptive and thought-provoking stories, but always with a touch of humor and wit. Her debut short film, Jasper, premiered at Palm Beach Atlantic University's Cinema Showcase in April 2024.
On the set of Jasper, Match Cut Cinema's latest short film. It showcases one man's quest for revenge, and the surprising choice he finds himself making.
